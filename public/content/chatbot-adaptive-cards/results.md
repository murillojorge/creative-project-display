## Proof of Concept Prototype Results

Our POC prototype demonstrates how structured data could be presented through adaptive cards in next-generation chatbot interfaces. While final implementation results are pending, our prototype provides valuable insights into potential improvements.

### Prototype Observations

**Enhanced Visual Presentation:**
The adaptive card format allows for structured presentation of product information with clear visual hierarchy, making complex data more digestible compared to traditional text-based responses.

**Interactive Elements:**
Our prototype incorporates interactive components that would allow users to explore product details, compare options, and take actions directly within the chat interface.

**Data Structure Implications:**
The prototype reveals key requirements for structuring API data to enable optimal rendering in adaptive card formats across different chatbot platforms.
